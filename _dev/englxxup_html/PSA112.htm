112
1 Alleluia.
Praise the Lord, ye servants of his,
Praise the name of the Lord.
2 Let the name of the Lord be blessed,
From this present time and forever.
3 From the rising of the sun to his setting,
The name of the Lord is to be praised.
4 The Lord is high above all the nations;
His glory is above the heavens.
5 Who is as the Lord our God?
Who dwells in the high places,
6 And yet looks upon the low things in heaven,
And on the earth:
7 Who lifts up the poor from the earth,
And raises up the needy from the dunghill;
8 To set him with princes,
Even with the princes of his people:
9 Who settles the barren woman in a house,
As a mother rejoicing over children.
