120
1 A Song of Degrees.
I lifted up mine eyes to the mountains,
Whence my help shall come.
2 My help shall come from the Lord,
Who made the heaven and the earth.
3 Let not thy foot be moved;
And let not thy keeper slumber.
4 Behold, he that keeps Israel
Shall not slumber nor sleep.
5 The Lord shall keep thee:
The Lord is thy shelter upon thy right hand.
6 The sun shall not burn thee by day,
Neither the moon by night.
7 May the Lord preserve thee from all evil:
The Lord shall keep thy soul.
8 The Lord shall keep thy coming in, and thy going out,
From henceforth and even forever.
