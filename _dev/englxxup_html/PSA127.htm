127
1 A Song of Degrees.
Blessed are all they that fear the Lord;
Who walk in his ways.
2 Thou shalt eat the labors of thy hands:
Blessed art thou, and it shall be well with thee.
3 Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine
On the sides of thy house:
Thy children as young olive plants
Round about thy table.
 
4 Behold, thus shall the man be blessed that fears the Lord.
5 May the Lord bless thee out of Zion;
And mayest thou see the prosperity of Jerusalem
All the days of thy life.
6 And mayest thou see thy children’s children.
Peace be upon Israel.
