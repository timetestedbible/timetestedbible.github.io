# Flood Event Sequence

## Overview

The Flood narrative in Genesis 7-8 provides precise day-by-day dating, allowing reconstruction of the entire sequence relative to the flood's start.

## Chain Structure

```
flood-begins (anchor: 2/17)
    ↓ -7 days
noah-enters-ark (2/10)
    ↓ +5 months from flood-begins
ark-rests-ararat (7/17)
    ↓ +2 months 14 days
mountain-tops-seen (10/1)
    ↓ +40 days
noah-sends-raven (11/10)
    ↓ +1 day
noah-sends-dove-first (11/11)
    ↓ +7 days
noah-sends-dove-second (11/18)
    ↓ +7 days
noah-sends-dove-third (11/25)

flood-begins + 1 year
    ↓
waters-dried-up (1/1 of next year)
    ↓ +1 month 26 days
earth-dried (2/27)
```

## Duration Details

| Event | Date | Reference | Source |
|-------|------|-----------|--------|
| Noah enters ark | 2/10 | flood-begins - 7d | Gen 7:4, 10 "For yet seven days..." |
| Flood begins | 2/17 | anchor | Gen 7:11 "second month, 17th day" |
| Ark rests on Ararat | 7/17 | flood-begins + 5m | Gen 8:4 "seventh month, 17th day" |
| Mountain tops seen | 10/1 | ark-rests + 2m14d | Gen 8:5 "tenth month, first day" |
| Raven sent | 11/10 | mountain-tops + 40d | Gen 8:6-7 "at the end of 40 days" |
| Dove sent (1st) | 11/11 | raven + 1d | Gen 8:8 implicit next day |
| Dove sent (2nd) | 11/18 | dove-1st + 7d | Gen 8:10 "yet other seven days" |
| Dove sent (3rd) | 11/25 | dove-2nd + 7d | Gen 8:12 "yet other seven days" |
| Waters dried up | 1/1 (year 2) | flood-begins + 1y | Gen 8:13 "first month, first day" |
| Earth dried | 2/27 | waters-dried + 1m26d | Gen 8:14 "second month, 27th day" |

## Notes

- Total time in ark: ~1 year and 10 days (2/17 to 2/27 next year)
- The 5 months from 2/17 to 7/17 = 150 days (Gen 7:24), indicating 30-day months
