# 49 — Number Study

## Symbolic Meaning

| Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **One Word** | Jubilee |
| **Two Words** | Perfected Rest |
| **Sentence** | The number of intensified spiritual perfection—representing the completion before Jubilee, the Father's perfect love, and the consummation of God's redemptive purposes before release and restoration |

**Opposite**: Bondage / Unreleased / Incomplete rest

---

## The Argument

### 1. Seven Times Seven: Perfection Squared

> **Forty-nine = 7 × 7**

Seven represents spiritual perfection and completion. Seven times seven INTENSIFIES this—perfection multiplied by perfection. It represents the ultimate completion of a cycle.

### 2. Forty-Nine Before Jubilee

> **Leviticus 25:8-10** — "And thou shalt number **seven sabbaths of years** unto thee, **seven times seven years**; and the space of the seven sabbaths of years shall be unto thee **forty and nine years**. Then shalt thou cause the trumpet of the jubile to sound on the tenth day of the seventh month... And ye shall hallow the **fiftieth year**, and proclaim liberty throughout all the land"

FORTY-NINE years lead to the FIFTIETH—the Jubilee of freedom, restoration, and release. Forty-nine is the completion of the cycle, the fullness of time before liberation.

### 3. The Time Before Pentecost

> **Leviticus 23:15-16** — "ye shall count unto you from the morrow after the sabbath... **seven sabbaths** shall be complete: Even unto the morrow after the seventh sabbath shall ye number **fifty days**"

FORTY-NINE days (seven sevens) are counted before Pentecost (day 50). The period of waiting, preparation, and anticipation.

### 4. The Consummation of Waiting

Forty-nine represents the threshold moment:
- The last day before freedom
- The completion of preparation
- The fullness of anticipation
- Everything ready for release

## Summary

**FORTY-NINE** symbolizes **PERFECTED REST AND CONSUMMATION**—the completion of seven sabbatical cycles before Jubilee, the fullness of preparation before Pentecost. As 7 × 7, it represents spiritual perfection intensified to its ultimate degree. Forty-nine is the threshold of release—everything complete, freedom about to dawn.
