# 12 — Number Study

## Symbolic Meaning

| Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **One Word** | Government |
| **Two Words** | Divine Authority |
| **Sentence** | The number of governmental perfection and God's ordered rule—representing divine authority exercised through chosen representatives, the complete structure of God's people under His headship |

**Opposite**: Disorder / Anarchy / Incomplete authority (11)

---

## The Argument

### 1. Twelve Tribes of Israel

> **Genesis 49:28** — "All these are the **twelve tribes** of Israel"

> **Exodus 24:4** — "Moses... builded an altar under the hill, and **twelve pillars**, according to the **twelve tribes** of Israel"

> **Revelation 21:12** — "twelve gates... names written thereon, which are the names of the **twelve tribes**"

The TWELVE tribes form God's complete governmental structure for His Old Testament people.

### 2. Twelve Apostles

> **Matthew 10:1-4** — "he had called unto him his **twelve disciples**"

> **Luke 6:13** — "of them he chose **twelve**, whom also he named apostles"

> **Revelation 21:14** — "the wall of the city had **twelve foundations**, and in them the names of the **twelve apostles**"

The TWELVE apostles form the governmental foundation of the Church—mirroring the twelve tribes.

### 3. Mathematical Significance: 3 × 4

**Twelve = Three (divine) × Four (earth/creation)**

When divine authority (3) is multiplied by universal scope (4), the result is governmental perfection (12):
- God's rule (3) over all the earth (4)
- Divine order applied universally

### 4. Twelve in Time and Space

| Element | Significance |
|---------|--------------|
| 12 months | Complete year/time cycle |
| 12 hours (day) | Complete daylight period |
| 12 hours (night) | Complete night period (Jn 11:9) |
| 12 zodiac signs | Complete heavenly circuit |

God's governance structures TIME itself in twelves.

### 5. The New Jerusalem: Twelves Everywhere

> **Revelation 21:12-21**:
- **12 gates** with 12 angels
- **12 tribes** named on gates
- **12 foundations** with 12 apostle names
- **12,000 furlongs** in each dimension
- **12 pearls** (gates)
- **12 fruits** on tree of life (Rev 22:2)

The eternal city is measured in TWELVES—perfect government forever.

### 6. Twelve Loaves of Showbread

> **Leviticus 24:5** — "thou shalt take fine flour, and bake **twelve cakes** thereof"

TWELVE loaves in the Tabernacle—representing the twelve tribes before God continually.

## Twelve Baskets Remaining

> **Matthew 14:20** — "they took up of the fragments that remained **twelve baskets** full"

After feeding the 5,000 (Jews), TWELVE baskets remained—one for each tribe. God's provision for His governmental people is complete.

Compare with:

> **Mark 8:8** — After feeding the 4,000 (Gentiles), **seven** baskets remained—spiritual perfection for the nations.

## Summary

**TWELVE** symbolizes **GOVERNMENTAL PERFECTION**—God's ordered rule through His chosen structures. Twelve tribes, twelve apostles, twelve gates, twelve foundations—all represent the complete authority of divine government. As 3 × 4, twelve unites divine nature with universal scope: God ruling over all creation through His appointed order.
