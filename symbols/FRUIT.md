# FRUIT — Symbol Study

## Symbolic Meaning

| Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **One Word** | Works |
| **Two Words** | Deeds / Actions |
| **Sentence** | The visible works, deeds, and actions that result from and reveal the nature of their source—as a tree is known by its fruit, so people are known by their works |

**Opposite**: Barren / Fruitless

---

## The Explicit Parallel: Works = Fruit (Galatians 5)

| Verse | Term |
|-------|------|
| **Galatians 5:19** | "the **WORKS** of the flesh are..." |
| **Galatians 5:22** | "the **FRUIT** of the Spirit is..." |

WORKS and FRUIT are parallel terms!

---

## Key Proof Texts

- **Matthew 7:16,20** — "By their **fruits** ye shall know them"
- **John 15:5** — "bringeth forth much **fruit**: for without me ye can **do nothing**"
- **Matthew 3:8** — "Bring forth **fruits** meet for repentance" (works proving repentance)

---

## "Fruit Of" = "Result/Product Of"

| Phrase | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Fruit of the Spirit | Works of the Spirit |
| Fruit of the mouth | Results of speech |
| Fruit of the womb | Children (result of conception) |
| Fruit of righteousness | Works of righteousness |

---

## Connection to Other Symbols

- **TREE** = Nation → **FRUIT** = Nation's works
- **THORNS** = Worldly → Choke out fruit/works (Mark 4:7)

---

## Summary

**FRUIT** = **WORKS / DEEDS**

Galatians 5 proves it by using "works" and "fruit" as parallels. Bearing fruit = doing works of obedience.
