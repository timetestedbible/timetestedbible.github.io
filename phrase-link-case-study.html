Key Observations
What makes this thematic, not just lexical
# The AI identifies connections that pure word-matching would MISS:

1. "birth pains" (Mat) ↔ "pangs...woman in travail" (Jer)
   Different words, same imagery. Greek ωδιν ≠ Hebrew חבל/ילד
   but they express the same prophetic metaphor.

2. "nation against nation" (Mat) ↔ "ruler against ruler" (Jer)
   The PATTERN "X against X" is the link, not the specific nouns.
   No shared root words at all — purely structural parallel.

3. "earthquakes" (Mat) ↔ "the earth is shaken" (Jer/Isa)
   Greek σεισμος (noun) ↔ Hebrew רעש (verb) — different languages,
   different parts of speech, same concept.

4. "not alarmed" (Mat) ↔ "heart grow faint" (Jer) ↔ "hands feeble" (Jer) ↔ "paleness" (Jer)
   FOUR different Hebrew/Greek words all expressing the same theme: fear-response.
   θροεω ≠ לבב ≠ רפה ≠ ירקון — but AI recognizes the thematic unity.

5. Stars/sun/moon (Isa 13) → Mat 24:29 (not in our passages but AI would link it)
   AI knows Mat 24:29 continues the Olivet Discourse with identical celestial imagery.
   The phrase link graph extends BEYOND the passages we're analyzing.
