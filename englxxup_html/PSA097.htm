97
1 A Psalm of David.
Sing to the Lord a new song;
For the Lord has wrought wonderful works,
His right hand, and his holy arm,
Have wrought salvation for him.
 
2 The Lord has made known his salvation,
He has revealed his righteousness in the sight of the nations.
3 He has remembered his mercy to Jacob,
And his truth to the house of Israel;
All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.
 
4 Shout to God, all the earth;
Sing, and exult, and sing psalms.
5 Sing to the Lord with a harp,
With a harp, and the voice of a psalm.
6 With trumpets of metal, and the sound of a trumpet of horn
Make a joyful noise to the Lord before the king.
7 Let the sea be moved, and the fullness of it;
The world, and they that dwell in it.
8 The rivers shall clap their hands together;
The mountains shall exult.
9 For he is come to judge the earth;
He shall judge the world in righteousness,
And the nations in uprightness.
