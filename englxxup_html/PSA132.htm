132
1 A Song of Degrees.
See now! what is so good, or what so pleasant,
As for brethren to dwell together?
2 It is as ointment on the head, that ran down to the beard,
Even the beard of Aaron;
That ran down to the fringe of his clothing.
3 As the dew of Hermon, that comes down on the mountains of Zion:
For there the Lord commanded the blessing,
Even life forever.
