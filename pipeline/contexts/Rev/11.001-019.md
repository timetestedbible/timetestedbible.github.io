## Recommended Studies

- **GOSPEL-OF-KINGDOM** — V7 "finished their testimony" pivots the entire chapter. The study reveals G5055 teleo derives from G5056 telos and that the beast CANNOT act until testimony completes — the witness period is protected. This reframes Rev 11 from persecution narrative to completion narrative: witnesses aren't victims, they're finishers. The teleo→telos chain (Mat 24:14 → Rev 11:7 → Rev 10:7 → 1Cor 15:24) is the structural spine connecting the Olivet Discourse to the trumpet sequence.

- **SACKCLOTH** — V3 "clothed in sackcloth" appears to be mourning garb, but the study reveals sackcloth = visible testimony of judgment, and the same word covers the sun (Rev 6:12). The smoke-mechanism (Rev 9:2) shows sackcloth IS what Babylon's burning looks like from below. Reframes the witnesses' clothing from "in grief" to "inside the smoke of Babylon's collapse" — they prophesy through the very fire that darkens the world.

- **PIT-ABYSS** — V7 "beast from bottomless pit" sounds like demonic origin, but the study reveals pit = enforced containment removing influence, always temporary and openable (≠ lake of fire). Beast EMERGES from where it was held — influence restored after containment. Isa 24:22 "after many days visited" = Rev 20:1-3 identical structure. Reframes the beast from a new monster to a released entity whose containment period ended.

- **NAME** — V18 "them that fear thy name" + v19 "ark of his testament" converge: the NAME study shows 2Sam 7:13 God substitutes NAME for ARK of the covenant. Ark revealed = covenant unveiled = NAME made known. Reframes v19 from temple-furniture vision to the covenant itself becoming visible — what was hidden behind the veil is now exposed at the 7th trumpet.

## Phase 2 Propagation

**ISA 34 → PERMANENT SMOKE ORIGIN.** Isa 34:10 "smoke ascends forever" is the OT source-text for Rev 14:11 + Rev 19:3. Rev 11:3 witnesses prophesy 1,260 days inside this smoke (sackcloth study: sackcloth = smoke's appearance). The smoke chain runs Isa 34:10 → Rev 9:2 (pit-smoke darkens sun) → Rev 11:3 (witnesses clothed in sackcloth/smoke) → Rev 18:9 (kings see Babylon's smoke) → Rev 19:3 (smoke forever). Isa 34's cherem-sacrifice (v6 blood of lambs/goats) parallels Rev 11:18 "destroy the destroyers" — both are ritual judicial executions, not military victories.

**AMOS 8:9-12 → FAMINE-OF-WORD = WITNESSES KILLED.** Sackcloth study traces: Amos 8:9 sun dark → v10 sackcloth → v11 famine of word → v12 seek and not find. Rev 11:7 witnesses killed = Amos 8:11 word withdrawn = famine begins. The Amos 8 context confirms this is the MECHANISM: testimony ceases → sun (authority of God's word) goes dark → sackcloth covers both sun and people simultaneously. Rev 11:3+7 is Amos 8:9-12 enacted in apocalyptic register.

**ISA 24:22-23 → PIT-TO-KINGDOM = REV 11 STRUCTURE.** Isa 24:22 authorities imprisoned in pit (H953 bor) → "after many days visited" → v23 "LORD shall REIGN in mount Zion." Rev 11: beast ascends FROM pit (v7) → 7th trumpet → "kingdoms become our Lord's" (v15). Same structural arc: pit-containment → release → kingdom-transfer. Isa 24 context notes v23 IS Rev 11:15 = 7th trumpet from the OT.

**JOEL 3:13-16 → HARVEST-DARKENING-ROAR = 7TH TRUMPET CLUSTER.** Joel 3:15-16 sun/moon dark + heavens/earth shake + LORD roars from Zion. This is darkening oracle #8 (sun-moon-stars study), notable because the darkening is SIMULTANEOUS with kingdom-assertion — old authorities extinguished at the exact moment the true authority speaks. Rev 11:15-19 replicates: earthquake (v19) + kingdom declared (v15) in one event. Joel 3:13 sickle/harvest links to Rev 11:18 "time of the dead that they should be judged" — the harvest IS the judgment at the 7th trumpet.

**DAN 5 → BABYLON-FEAST-DEATH = REV 11:10 INVERTED.** Dan 5 context traces: feast → drink → death that night (Jer 51:39 enacted). Rev 11:10 earth-dwellers "make merry and send gifts" over dead witnesses — a FEAST celebrating the silenced testimony. But Rev 11:11 Spirit of life enters them → Rev 11:13 earthquake → judgment. The feasters become the judged. Dan 5's Belshazzar feast pattern inverted: in Dan 5, Babylon feasts and dies; in Rev 11, Babylon feasts over dead witnesses and STILL dies.
