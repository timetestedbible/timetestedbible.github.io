## SOURCE TEXT FOR 1TH 5:3

This 3-verse unit is the OT origin of Paul's "peace and safety → sudden destruction" (1Th 5:3). The chain: Jer 6:14 → Jer 8:11 (verbatim repeat) → Ezek 13:10,16 → Mic 3:5 → 1Th 5:3. Each link escalates from local to cosmic.

## H7667 SHEBER + H7043 QALAL — WOUND DIAGNOSIS

v14: "healed the HURT [H7667 sheber = fracture/shattering] of the daughter of my people SLIGHTLY [H7043 qalal = lightly/superficially]." The wound is H7667 = structural break (same word as shipwreck, broken vessel). The treatment is H7043 = trivialize, make light of. Medical metaphor: covenant is FRACTURED; prophets apply bandage and declare healing. False diagnosis ≠ no wound. The fracture remains; the declaration changes nothing. This IS the false-peace mechanism.

## THREE H7965 IN ONE VERSE

v14 uses shalom 3×: claimed twice ("shalom, shalom"), denied once ("no shalom"). The doubled claim against the single reality. Isa 26:3 uses the same doubling ("shalom shalom") for GENUINE covenant-wholeness — mind stayed on God. Same words, opposite truth-value. The doubling in Jer 6:14 is a COUNTERFEIT of Isa 26:3's reality.

## VERBATIM REPEAT — JER 8:10-12

Jer 8:10-12 reproduces 6:13-15 nearly word-for-word. NOT editorial error — deliberate structural bracketing. Two witnesses to the same indictment. Between them (chs 7-8): temple sermon, burning incense to Baal, children in fire at Topheth. The false-peace proclamation bookends Judah's worst covenant violations. The prophets saying "shalom" WHILE the people sacrifice children.

## COVETOUSNESS CHAIN — LEAST TO GREATEST (v13)

H1215 betsa = unjust gain/covetousness. From H1214 batsa = to cut off, break off, plunder. "From the least to the greatest" = TOTAL infection, no exceptions. Prophet + priest included — the very offices charged with maintaining covenant are compromised. FAMINE study: when Word-bearers deal falsely, the Word is withdrawn → spiritual famine.

## UNABLE TO BLUSH (v15)

H954 bosh = ashamed + H3637 kalam = humiliated, put to confusion. "Neither COULD they blush" — not that they chose not to, but that they LOST THE CAPACITY. Shame = covenant mechanism that signals violation. Loss of shame = severed feedback loop. System cannot self-correct → judgment becomes necessary. Same mechanism: Rom 1:21-32 "gave them over" — capacity for recognition removed.

## H3782 KASHAL — STUMBLE/CAST DOWN (v15b)

"They shall FALL [H5307 naphal] among them that fall; at the time I VISIT [H6485 paqad] them they shall be CAST DOWN [H3782 kashal]." H3782 kashal = stumble, stagger, brought to ruin. Same root as G4625 skandalon (STUMBLE study: trap-stick mechanism). The false-peace proclaimers become the first to fall. SUDDEN_DESTRUCTION: "at the time I visit" = H6485 paqad = appointed reckoning. The gap between false diagnosis and divine visitation = the space where judgment builds unseen.

## IMMEDIATE CONTEXT — vv16-19: REJECTED REMEDY

v16: "ask for the old paths, the good WAY" → they said "we will not walk therein." v17: "I set WATCHMEN... hearken to the trumpet" → "we will not hearken." v19: "fruit of their THOUGHTS... rejected my law." Three rejections in sequence: (1) refuse the WAY, (2) refuse the WARNING, (3) refuse the WORD. Each refusal = deeper covenant departure. v16 echoes Isa 59:8 "the WAY of shalom they know not." They claim shalom (v14) but refuse the path that produces it (v16). Shalom study: righteousness PRECEDES peace (Heb 7:2). No righteousness → no peace → "shalom shalom" = lie.

## ROADMAP: FALSE PEACE → SUDDEN DESTRUCTION SEQUENCE

This passage establishes the mechanism that 1Th 5:3 makes eschatological:
1. Covenant wound exists (fracture = broken relationship)
2. False prophets trivialize wound (superficial healing)
3. "Shalom" declared while covenant shattered
4. Capacity for shame lost (cannot self-correct)
5. Divine visitation = sudden reckoning

Paul in 1Th 5:3 applies this to the DAY OF THE LORD: "when they say PEACE and SAFETY → sudden destruction as birth pains." The Jer 6:14 pattern = prototype for the final false-peace moment before cosmic judgment. The false-peace proclaimers of every era run the same script.
