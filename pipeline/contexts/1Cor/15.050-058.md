## What the Pipeline Adds

**G3466 MYSTERION (v51):** "I shew you a G3466 mystery" — same word as 2Th 2:7 "mystery of iniquity" and Rev 17:5 "MYSTERY BABYLON." Paul's mystery = "we shall not all G2837 sleep, but we shall all be G236 changed." The mystery is NOT the resurrection itself (already argued vv1-49) — it's the TRANSFORMATION OF THE LIVING. Dead rise (known); living changed without dying (mystery). G236 allasso = exchange one thing for another. The living swap mortality for immortality without passing through death.

**G823 ATOMOS + G4493 RHIPĒ (v52):** "In a G823 moment (atomos = indivisible, from a+tomos=un-cuttable), in the G4493 twinkling (rhipē = a jerk/cast/rapid movement) of an G3788 eye." G823 atomos appears ONLY here in NT — the smallest possible time unit, literally un-dividable. MOMENT study: this is SUDDEN_DESTRUCTION's mirror — same instantaneity, opposite outcome. For the wicked: destruction in a moment (Isa 47:11 H7722+H6365 Babylon's ruin). For the covenant-faithful: transformation in an atomos. Same speed, opposite destinies — echoes 1Th 5:3-9 where sudden destruction falls on "them" while believers are appointed to salvation.

**"LAST TRUMP" G2078+G4536 (v52):** G2078 eschatos (last/uttermost) + G4536 salpinx (trumpet). RETURN_OF_CHRIST study anchors this: 7th trumpet (Rev 11:15) = "last trump" = "trump of God" (1Th 4:16) = "great trumpet" (Mat 24:31, Isa 27:13). Five-stream convergence at one event:
(1) 1Cor 15:52 — dead raised + living changed
(2) 1Th 4:16 — Lord descends + dead rise + caught up
(3) Mat 24:31 — angels + trumpet + gather elect from four winds
(4) Rev 11:15-18 — kingdoms become Lord's + dead judged + saints rewarded + destroyers destroyed
(5) Isa 27:13 — great trumpet + gathered from Assyria/Egypt (covenant ingathering)
ALL describe transformation + gathering + kingdom transfer AT the trumpet.

## Recommended Studies

- **THE_END** — v24 "then the telos" is routinely read as "the end of the world," but the study reveals G5056 telos = the GOAL/PURPOSE arriving (Rom 10:4 "Christ is the telos of Torah" = goal, not abolition). The telos arrives when Christ delivers the kingdom after putting down ALL authority (G2673 katargeo = same verb destroying the lawless one in 2Th 2:8). This reframes "the end" from catastrophe to COMPLETION — the covenant purpose achieved. The teleo/telos chain (Mat 24:14, Rev 11:7, Rev 10:7) shows: testimony FINISHED → telos arrives → kingdom transferred. The 7th trumpet IS the telos.

- **RETURN_OF_CHRIST** — v23 establishes resurrection ORDER (G5001 tagma = military rank): Christ firstfruits → THEN those at His parousia. The study reveals three Greek words for the same event from different angles: G3952 parousia (royal arrival), G2015 epiphaneia (visible shining), G602 apokalypsis (uncovering). All converge: the King's visible arrival IS the judgment IS the gathering. 2Th 2:8's "epiphaneia of his parousia" destroying the lawless one = the SAME event as 1Cor 15:23-24's parousia transforming the saints. One event, two outcomes — destruction for the lawless, transformation for the faithful.

- **THIRD_DAY** — v4 "raised the third day according to the scriptures" points to Hos 6:2, but the study traces the pattern from Gen 1:9-13 (third day: earth emerges from sea, vegetation appears = life from chaos) through Abraham/Isaac (Gen 22:4), Sinai (Exod 19:11), Jonah, to Christ. Every instance: death/judgment → third day → God acts decisively. If the day-as-1000-years principle applies, Christ's resurrection was ~2 "days" ago — the corporate "third day" harvest may be imminent.
