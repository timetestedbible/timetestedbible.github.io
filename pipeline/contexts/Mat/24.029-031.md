## Critical Corrections

Mat 24:31 "gather his elect from the four winds" = gathering FROM the domain where the four spirits/horsemen have been operating. The elect are gathered OUT of the territory the horsemen have been riding through. The winds that carried judgment now carry the gathering-angels.

**2. SEALING PRECONDITION (REV 7:1-4) GOVERNS THE ENTIRE SEQUENCE.** The four winds CANNOT harm any tree/sea/grass UNTIL God's servants are sealed (Rev 7:3 "till"). The birth pains of Mat 24:4-8 = the four horsemen = the four winds. They cannot begin until the sealing is complete. This means the sealing event is PRIOR to the entire Olivet Discourse sequence — it is the gate that opens the mechanism.

**3. WHO DESTROYS BABYLON THE GREAT — NOT AN EXTERNAL ENEMY.** Rev 17:16-17: the ten horns (political powers within the beast-system itself) hate the harlot, make her desolate, burn her with fire — because GOD put it in their hearts. V29's darkening (Isa 13:10 = Babylon oracle) = the moment Babylon's authority structure collapses. The smoke of that self-consuming fire (Ezek 28:18 "fire from the midst of thee") IS the cloud through which Christ appears in v30.

Citizens go OUT to meet arriving dignitary → escort Him BACK to His destination. Acts 28:15 demonstrates: brethren met Paul → escorted him INTO Rome. The gathering of v31 is an escort of the returning King toward earth, not departure from it.
