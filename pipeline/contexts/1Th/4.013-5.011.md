## Phase 2 Propagation

**DAN 5 → LITERAL PROTOTYPE OF 1TH 5:2-7.** The Dan 5 context documents: Belshazzar's feast = drunk at night → kingdom falls → slain that night. Dan 5 context explicitly notes "Paul's eschatological framework in 1Th 5 uses the EXACT Babylon-fall-at-feast pattern." The chain: Jer 51:39 "make them drunken, sleep a perpetual sleep" → Dan 5 (enacted) → 1Th 5:6-7 "they that sleep sleep in the night, they that be drunken are drunken in the night." The PERPETUAL_SLEEP study confirms the three-stream convergence: Babylon-feast (Dan 5), eschatological-night (1Th 5), wisdom-drunkard (Pro 23:31-35). Paul isn't using generic metaphor — he's citing Babylon's literal death pattern.

**ISA 47:8-11 → "PEACE AND SAFETY" = "I AM, AND NONE ELSE."** The sudden-destruction study traces G3639 olethros → LXX Isa 47:11 (H7722+H6365). 1Th 5:3 "when they shall say, Peace and safety" = Isa 47:8 "I am, and none else beside me; I shall not sit as a widow." Same posture: absolute false security immediately before sudden destruction. Isa 47 context adds: "in a MOMENT, in ONE DAY" (v9) = template for Rev 18:8,10 "one hour." The vocabulary Paul uses isn't pastoral metaphor — it's Babylon oracle terminology repurposed for eschatological warning.

**AMOS 8:9-12 → WORD-WITHDRAWN = DARKNESS DESCENDS.** 1Th 5:4-5 "ye are not in darkness... ye are children of light and of the day" gains depth through Amos 8. The Amos 8 context maps: sun dark (v9) → sackcloth (v10) → famine of the word (v11) → seek and not find (v12). DARKNESS in 1Th 5 isn't merely moral — it's the condition of living in the famine-of-the-word era when testimony has been withdrawn. Believers who are "of the day" = those who have the word WHILE it's available. The sackcloth study confirms: Rev 11:3 witnesses prophesy during the smoke/sackcloth period, then v7 they're killed = Amos 8:11 word withdrawn = darkness complete.

**JER 51:45-64 → "COME OUT" = EXTRACTION BEFORE FALL.** Jer 51:45 "my people, go ye out" → Rev 18:4 (verbatim) → 1Th 4:17 "caught up." The Jer 51 context documents: COME-OUT chain = extraction before Babylon's sudden destruction. 1Th 4:17 apantēsis (civic escort) is the NT mechanism for the OT "come out" command. The rapture-gathering study's H6908 qabats = God regathering scattered covenant people FROM among nations. Paul's "caught up to meet the Lord" is Jer 51:45 enacted at cosmic scale — extraction from the Babylon-system before its perpetual-sleep judgment falls.

**2PET 3:1-13 → CONFIRMS ENTIRE 1TH 5 FRAMEWORK.** The 2Pet 3 context explicitly catalogs the parallels: thief-in-night (2Pet 3:10 = 1Th 5:2), sudden destruction (2Pet 3:7 = 1Th 5:3), sleep/scoffers (2Pet 3:3-4 = 1Th 5:6-7), watchful exemption (both). Peter adds two elements absent from Paul: (1) DAY_AS_1000_YEARS bidirectionality (v8) explaining the parousia "delay," (2) FIRE as judgment mechanism (vv7,10,12) escalating from Noah's water. The water→fire escalation (days-of-noah study) places 1Th 5's sudden destruction in the fire-era, not the water-era.
