- 2Thess 2:3 — man of sin = "son of perdition" — SAME TITLE, same function
- Rev 17:8 — beast "shall ascend out of the bottomless pit, and go into perdition"
- Rev 17:11 — beast "is the eighth, and is of the seven, and goeth into perdition"
- 2Pet 3:7 — "day of judgment and perdition of ungodly men"

Three-point link: Judas (betrayal) ↔ man of sin (usurpation) ↔ beast (system-incarnation) — all share G684 destiny. Betrayal-from-within-covenant-space principle.
