# 1000 — Number Study

## Symbolic Meaning

| Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **One Word** | Magnitude |
| **Two Words** | Divine Immensity |
| **Sentence** | The number of divine magnitude and immensity—representing vast quantities, God's greatness beyond human measure, and the completeness of His eternal purposes |

**Opposite**: Small / Limited / Countable

---

## The Argument

### 1. A Thousand Years as One Day

> **Psalm 90:4** — "For **a thousand years** in thy sight are but as yesterday when it is past, and as a watch in the night"

> **2 Peter 3:8** — "one day is with the Lord as **a thousand years**, and **a thousand years** as one day"

ONE THOUSAND years represents divine perspective on time—what seems vast to us is a moment to God.

### 2. The Millennial Reign

> **Revelation 20:2-4** — "bound him **a thousand years**... they lived and reigned with Christ **a thousand years**"

Christ's kingdom reign = ONE THOUSAND years. Complete divine rule on earth.

### 3. A Thousand Generations

> **Deuteronomy 7:9** — "the faithful God, which keepeth covenant and mercy with them that love him and keep his commandments **to a thousand generations**"

> **Psalm 105:8** — "He hath remembered his covenant for ever, the word which he commanded **to a thousand generations**"

ONE THOUSAND generations = covenant faithfulness beyond measure.

### 4. Mathematical Significance

**1000 = 10 × 10 × 10 = Order cubed**

Ten (complete order) raised to the third power = divine magnitude of completeness.

### 5. Thousands Upon Thousands

> **Daniel 7:10** — "**thousand thousands** ministered unto him, and ten **thousand times ten thousand** stood before him"

> **Revelation 5:11** — "**ten thousand times ten thousand**, and **thousands of thousands**"

Countless heavenly hosts—1000s multiplied express God's immeasurable realm.

Complete governmental perfection (144) magnified to divine scale (×1000).

## Summary

**ONE THOUSAND** symbolizes **DIVINE MAGNITUDE**—quantities beyond human comprehension, God's immense faithfulness, and complete eras of His purposes. A thousand years are as one day to the Lord. Christ reigns a thousand years. God keeps covenant to a thousand generations. As 10³, it represents order perfected to the third degree—completeness magnified infinitely.
